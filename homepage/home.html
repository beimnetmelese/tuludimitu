About Us

Tullu Dimtu Primary School is renowned for offering exceptional education to students in grades 1 through 8. We prioritize a student-centered learning approach that fosters creativity and skill development. Our experienced teachers bring a wealth of knowledge to the classroom, enhancing the learning experience. Our mission is to cultivate a foundation of intellectual, ethical, and creative growth in our students, empowering them to contribute positively to society and build a better world.
